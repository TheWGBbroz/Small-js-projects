'Rocket' evolution

The ellipses are the rockets. The green ellipse is the rocket which has the average genes, which I thought was interesting to add.
The average rocket can't reproduce.
The obstacles (the pink ellipses) are placed randomly and if a rocket collides with it (or the boundaries) it gets stuck.
A rockets fitness value is 1 devided by the distance from the rocket to the target point (red ellipse).
If a rocket gets stuck it's fitness value is multiplied by 0.2.
Rockets with high fitness values have a low chance of dying, and the rockets with low fitness values have a high chance of dying.
After 10 seconds the rockets which are alive get to reproduce, with a mutation value applied.
